## Project Overview

**BeCompass** — Berau Coal Operational Monitoring, Predictive Analytics & Safety System.

The product concept: technology positioned not as surveillance or extra workload, but as a safety compass (penunjuk arah keselamatan) guiding field workers through their shift. The name and framing deliberately evoke the compass as a familiar tool in mining and field operations.
